About Us

Kya Kya Surabaya Is Surabaya's beloved Chinatown, rich not only in history but also culture. Here you will find the oldest city of Surabaya, once occupied by the chinese public since the 1400s now shine bright with festivities as far as the eye can see.

Just like it's namesake Kya Kya which means "Taking a Walk" in chinese, this spot is a great place for tourists and locals alike to stroll around and enjoy everything it has to offer starting from the delicious assortments of chinese & surabaya inspired dishes, the traditional attraction guaranteed to knock your socks off, the absolutely stunning architectural works of our predecessors full of historic significance and many more for you and your family to explore.
